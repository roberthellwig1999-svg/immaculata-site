Academic Excellence

Under the leadership of Mrs. Kristin Boczany, Vice Principal of Academic Affairs, our rigorous curriculum prepares students for success in college and beyond through Advanced Placement, Honors, and College Preparatory courses.

Advanced Placement

Over 18 AP courses across all disciplines, from Calculus BC and Physics C to Literature and Art History, providing college-level rigor and the opportunity to earn college credit.

Honors Program

Accelerated coursework for high-achieving students, fostering critical thinking, research skills, and intellectual curiosity through challenging curriculum and independent study opportunities.

Technology Integration

Our 1:1 Chromebook program ensures every student has access to modern educational technology, preparing them for the digital demands of college and career success.
